About Boubli Club

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Boubli Club was born from the passion of a group of students for video content creation and organizing memorable events.

Today, we have become one of the most dynamic university clubs, with an engaged and creative community.

Our Mission

To train the next generation of content creators while creating unforgettable memories through innovative and inclusive events.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the club?

Simply fill out our online membership form. Our team will contact you within 48 hours for an interview.

Are there membership fees?

The annual membership is 50 TND, providing access to all equipment, training, and exclusive events.

Do I need experience?

Not at all! We welcome all levels. Beginner training sessions are organized regularly.

How much time commitment?

We recommend 6-8 hours per week, but it's flexible depending on your availability and projects.
